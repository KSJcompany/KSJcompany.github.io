## Yonsei-Nexon RC Creative Platform (Apr 2023 – Nov 2023)

As the team leader in the Yonsei-Nexon RC Creative Platform, I led a project titled "Debugger helloWORLD: Transforming the World for the Visually Impaired." Our goal was to develop a braille translation program that could recognize braille from photos and translate the content into Korean. 

We were inspired by the fact that more than half of braille installations in public places are either incorrectly installed or follow improper standards in terms of braille format, content, or positioning. Our solution aimed to allow anyone, even those unfamiliar with braille, to easily identify these errors and report them to the relevant authorities.

### Project Development Timeline:

- **First Semester:** We focused on selecting and refining our project topic, establishing a timeline, and planning the project. 
- **Summer Vacation:** We collected data from public institutions and transportation systems with braille installations, and began initial coding.
- **Second Semester:** We completed the coding process, integrated individual contributions, and finalized the program. We also prepared posters and reports, and tested and revised the final program.

### Program Functionality:

1. The program adjusts the image’s angle and perspective to flatten the photograph.
2. It processes the image to highlight braille, making extraction easier.
3. The program identifies the braille and extracts the coordinates of each dot.
4. It analyzes the coordinates to convert the braille into braille ASCII.
5. The braille ASCII is then translated into Korean text.

During the project, we identified some braille installations in public places that were incorrectly implemented. While we were proud of our progress, one limitation was that we focused primarily on functionality and did not develop the program into a web or mobile app.
