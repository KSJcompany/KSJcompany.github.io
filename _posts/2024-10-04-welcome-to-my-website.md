I have created this academic website to document and share my ongoing **AI-related activities**. This website serves as a platform to present various academic pursuits, including project work, paper reviews, and participation in academic programs.

## Purpose of the Website
The primary goal of this website is to provide a space where I can showcase my contributions to the field of artificial intelligence. It will feature detailed write-ups of my projects, reviews of research papers, and insights from various AI-related activities. The content will be **regularly updated** to reflect my growth and learning in this field.

## Technology Stack
This website was built using **Jekyll** and hosted on **GitHub Pages**. Jekyll allows me to easily publish content, while GitHub Pages offers a reliable platform for hosting and managing my academic work.

## Development Process
During the website development process, I encountered a few technical challenges, especially with configuring the site to display specific sections, such as the bibliography and project pages. These challenges helped me enhance my web development skills and improve the site's functionality.

## Future Content
I will continue to update this site with:
- Reviews of research papers on various AI topics
- Descriptions and reports of my AI projects
- Insights from academic programs and events I participate in
