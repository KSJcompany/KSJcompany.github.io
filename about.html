About

I am an undergraduate student in the Department of Artificial Intelligence at Yonsei University, with a focus on computer vision and medical AI. My research interests lie at the intersection of artificial intelligence and healthcare, particularly in developing predictive models for disease diagnosis using biomedical data.


Current Research Activities:

  •   Member of YAI (Yonsei Artificial Intelligence) academic society
  •   Analyzing biomedical data for disease prediction
  •   Exploring applications of computer vision in medical imaging

Ongoing Projects:

  •   Participation in and exploration of projects related to AI-driven healthcare solutions
  •   Participation in competitions related to medical AI

Research Goals:

  •   Advance the integration of AI techniques in healthcare diagnostics
  •   Contribute to the development of innovative models for early disease detection
  •   Explore the potential of AI in improving patient care and medical outcomes

I am committed to research that connects AI and medical technologies, aiming to contribute to the advancement of medical technology and the utilization of biomedical data through AI.
